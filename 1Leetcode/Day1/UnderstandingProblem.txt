** Problem Understanding

What is substring : 
A continuous part of the string.

abc
bca
cab

are substrings.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------


What is subsequence :
A non continuous part of the string.


----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

                   ** What is the Solution ?

The Longest Substring Without repeating of characters.
